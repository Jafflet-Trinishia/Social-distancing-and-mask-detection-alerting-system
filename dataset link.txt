The dataset used comprises 7,959 images containing
 specific images from WIDER-FACE and MAFA datasets, 
with facial annotations belonging to two classes, 
masked faces and unmasked faces.
        1. http://shuoyang1213.me/WIDERFACE/
        2. https://www.kaggle.com/rahulmangalampalli
/mafa-data

After verification of wrong annotations, 
6,120 images were used for training and 
1,839 images were set aside for validation.
